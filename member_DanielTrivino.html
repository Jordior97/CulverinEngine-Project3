My Job


Early in development I documented and made some concept drawings for each of the different props that would be used in the scenes that later on I would model. After the Vertical Slice 2 I managed to figure out how make PBR textures and made them for the previous props and added new ones I did. I animated some of those props too.

Later in development I was in charge of generating particles and setting up their systems on our engine. Lastly, I made the website layout and programmed it.

3D Modelling

There are many props throughout the game to make the places don’t feel empty that I modeled to set the game’s environment. These had to be low poly as they were not the player’s main focus.

Wall Banner by dibu13 on Sketchfab

PBR Texturing

After the Vertical Slice 1, a decision was made to change the game’s art style to a more realistic one. We would use PBR texturing which was completely new for me. After learning how to I started texturizing some of the game’s props.

Chain Tile by dibu13 on Sketchfab

Barrel Pack by dibu13 on Sketchfab

Lever by dibu13 on Sketchfab

3D Animation

For a brief period of time I was in charge make some prop animations as we saw some needed movement.

Particles

On the latter stages of our project I was in charge of making some of the game’s particles. I had to create new textures for the engine’s particle systems with Photoshop.

Later I had to set up these systems to achieve the desired aesthetics.

Website

Last but not least, I was in charge of the layout and some programming of our project's website. I used the Bootstrap library to make it responsive and more accessible from different devices. Hope you like it :)
